5-Min Intro

Never miss any event

You have a schedule specially designed for CUHK. All events and tasks are presented in a nice and clean way. How can you miss anything? You can say goodbye to those weired-looking schedules.

Not just a schedule

It's not a simple schedule: your schedule is connected to your friends' and the campus via social network. Have nothing to do one Friday afternoon? See what events you can join with just a click.

Saves your time of searching

You must know how difficult it is to find an event: you have to look at posters and emails, figure out the time and check if there is any time clash. Now you can have all available events shown on your timetable!

Always know what's going on

With the event market, you can browse all events on campus. If you see anything interesting, click your mouth and it will appear on your schedule. No need to deal with forms and type anything: it couldn't be simpler.

Never get lost in the campus

You can find the location for all the events, on a intelligent map. You can see where all events are held today, or look into a specific event. The map can tell you how to go from one event to another. It's hard to get lost!

Connect to your friends

If you have something that you want to share, post a note on your wall. If you want a private conversation with your friends, send a message. You can always find a way to connect to your friends.

Everything's on mobile too!

All those nice features are also on mobile. With WiFi covered nearly all CUHK, the whole campus is in your hand. All major platforms are supported: iOS, Android, Symbian S60 ver5 and Blackberry OS ver6.

... and why?

We are a group of people (geeks) who love Web 2.0 Applications. And believe that there can be a lot of improvements to CUHK's current information system. Technology should not be a barrier, it should make a better life. Interestingly, we have similar principles in designing the prodcut: put user experience first. During the development, we will check with each other to avoid any possible usability mistakes.

The current progress

The coding progress is almost finished. We plan to fully test and optimize the code and focus on user experience and documentations.

In the near future

We plan to participate in some innovation competitions to gain more experience and more feedbacks from other perspectives. We want to implement the idea in CUHK first.

In the faraway future

The final goal is to push this to the market and benefit more people. But everything is subject to change and there will be lots of obstacles.
